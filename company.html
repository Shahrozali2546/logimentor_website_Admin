The Company

LogiMentor

LogiMentor is a high-tech, privately held small/medium enterprise based in Livorno (Pisa), Italy, with years of expertise in high-performance FPGA design and Digital Signal Processing.

Established in March 2014, LogiMentor has grown rapidly with a strong focus on FPGAs and their applications. The company has developed a proprietary HDL Design Flow, along with innovative techniques and utilities, and built an extensive library of high-performance, fully verified HDL IP cores.

The products and services provided by LogiMentor cover a wide range, from comprehensive FPGA design services to HDL IP cores, custom designs, and IP cores for markets such as Radar/Sonar, Aerospace and Defense, Space, Radio Astronomy, Test and Measurement, and High-Performance Computing.

LogiMentor offers vendor-independent, "off-the-shelf" IP cores for FPGAs, optimized for speed, power, and resource efficiency. Each IP Core is carefully conceived, developed, verified, documented, released, and maintained through a rigorous proprietary design flow.

JOIN US

Working at LogiMentor

At LogiMentor, we expect our employees to be as flexible as possible in how they organize their work. Everyone at LogiMentor works towards achieving shared objectives and goals. While working here, you might be asked to design a floating-point FIR filter in VHDL, write the user guide for the same filter, test it on an FPGA, and more. The way you organize these tasks is up to you. Of course, for certain roles and responsibilities, we may ask you to join us in our offices. However, LogiMentor strongly supports remote work, allowing us to collaborate with talented individuals from around the world. We prioritize results and, as the company grows, we ensure that our employees grow with us by sharing profits and opportunities for professional development.
