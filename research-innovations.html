LogiMentor

LogiMentor founders and employees have extensive research experience, also demonstrated by their publication record. LOGIMENTOR has also strong collaborations with major research and academic institutes in Italy (University of Pisa, University of Rome, Aristotle University of Thessaloniki – Greece, the National Institute of Nuclear Physics (INAF), National Institute of Astrophysics (INFN) and others) and Europe.

We continue to expand our collaboration network through a variety of research projects and applications, on a regional, national, and European level. The company is a member of HiPEAC (www.hipeac.net), the European Network on High Performance and Embedded Architecture and Compilation. By participating in this network, we gain access to a large collaboration in new research projects, we gain access to highly qualified researchers, get access to the latest research developments, and influence the direction of computing systems research.

PHAST

Photonic-based AeroSpace Technologies

The PhAST – Photonic-based AeroSpace Technologies: This project focuses on the development of innovative photonic-based technologies aimed at advancing aerospace applications. From satellite propulsion diagnostic tools to ground station equipment, the project strives to introduce new concepts for satellite diagnostic automation and to create next-generation communication systems for ground stations.
