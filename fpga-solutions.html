FPGA SOLUTIONS

We develop and sell our proprietary FPGA IP CORES, vendor-independent, optimized for speed, power, and resource usage, allowing our customers to easily integrate them into their own systems. We customize our IP cores and design new ones to meet our customers' specific needs. We offer: more than 115 IPs, 7 different libraries, 110,000 lines of code with 40,000 lines of comments.

The IPs range from simple to complex functions, and are available as ready-to-use configurable systems, from basic FIFO IP Cores to a complete ready-to-use ATA Over Ethernet Bridge IP.

IP Cores Key Features & Deliverables

  • Vendor independent “off the shelf” VHDL IP cores for FPGAs (Xilinx, Intel, Lattice, and Microsemi)

  • Optimized in terms of speed, power, and resource usage

  • Architected, developed, verified, released, and maintained through a rigorous and efficient process

  • DO-254 and IEC-61508 V&V support available upon request

  • Factory adjustable parameters and parallelism

  • Math and DSP Cores available in both fixed-point and floating-point formats

  • VHDL-2008 support available upon request

  • AXI Wrapper available upon request

  • Encrypted source code (maximum reusability across multiple targets)

  • Synthesized netlist (lowest cost, targeted at a single FPGA, low-cost respin to other targets)

  • Functional VHDL self-checking testbench, for DSP modules, a golden reference floating point, and a bit-accurate fixed-point model

  • Full set of documentation

  • Verification and Validation Procedure suitable for safety-critical applications (optional)
