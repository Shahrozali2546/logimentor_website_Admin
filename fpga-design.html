LogiMentor

DESIGN FLOW

We offer FPGA Design services of the highest standard, backed by our engineering expertise. Our HDL code is entirely vendor-independent. To ensure maximum compatibility with the various EDA tools used by our customers, each project is meticulously defined, architected, developed, verified, released, and maintained through a rigorous and efficient process.

FPGA DESIGN

KEY STEP

  • Architectural design, requirements tracing, verification, and validation plan definition.

  • Proprietary VHDL coding standard.

  • Linting automatic analysis to enhance HDL code quality.

  • Version control system (SVN) to ensure complete tracking of changes.

  • RTL-level functional tests: self-checking with code coverage and requirements tracking.

  • Modular-based design for maximum code reusability.

  • Tcl script-based synthesis and implementation management.

  • Hardware tests are used to validate the implementation process.

  • Documentation: the code and entire project are fully documented, including automatic documentation generation for VHDL.

  • Maintenance bug list tracked and properly managed.

FPGA HDL

DESIGN

At LogiMentor, we primarily use VHDL as our Hardware Description Language, though we are also proficient in Verilog and System Verilog.

We have successfully worked with a wide range of FPGA/CPLD families, from the simple Intel MAX10 to the high-end Xilinx Ultrascale+ FPGAs and SoCs in our projects.

LogiMentor is well-versed in FPGAs and development tools from all major FPGA vendors, including Intel, Lattice, Microsemi, and Xilinx.

We guarantee a de-risked, high-quality project through our modern and proprietary HDL development flow, coding standards, and techniques. Our stringent design process, proprietary utilities, and comprehensive library of thoroughly verified HDL IP cores ensure the best outcomes.

DIGITAL SIGNAL

PROCESSING

LogiMentor can develop algorithms for complex digital signal processing applications using floating point tools like Matlab, Octave, and Scilab, then convert them into bit-accurate fixed-point models for implementation on FPGA.

We have an extensive collection of IP cores that operate in super sample rate mode, enabling us to process streaming data in parallel at frequencies higher than the internal clock.

We have developed proprietary algorithms and IP cores for applications in Radar/Sonar, Software Defined Radio, Astronomy, Video Processing, and High-Performance Computing.

SAFETY

CRITICAL DESIGN AND V&V

Simulation, documentation, and independent assessment of FPGA project requirements.

LogiMentor can assist customers during the Verification and Validation (V&V) phase, utilizing our expertise, proprietary tools, utilities, and scripts.

We have developed a robust V&V process and operate in compliance with Safety-Critical standards such as DO-254 and IEC-61508.

EMBEDDED

SW DESIGN

LogiMentor offers embedded software development as a standalone service, or integrated with our custom hardware and FPGA design services.

We specialize in bare-metal and embedded OS development, custom bootloaders, board support packages (drivers and APIs) for platforms such as Xilinx Zynq, Intel Cyclone V SoC, Microsemi SmartFusion2, NIOS II, and Microblaze.

Embedded application development in C, C++, and assembler.

VHDL

OPTIMIZATION

LogiMentor has successfully assisted our customers in enhancing the performance of their products without the need to redesign the hardware.

We offer timing closure support services, utilizing proprietary analysis tools and a highly efficient design methodology.

Linting tools (both proprietary scripts and commercial tools) are employed to analyze designs, identify Cross Clock Domain issues, and assess the overall quality of the source code and project.

HARDWARE

DESIGN SERVICES

From hardware architecture design to schematic creation, PCB design, and board bring-up.

LogiMentor has successfully designed and delivered prototype boards featuring FPGAs, SoC, ADCs, DACs, DDR, Flash, DDS, VHDL control firmware, and embedded software for both soft and hard embedded microprocessors.

Board Self-Test & Simulation Systems: LogiMentor has developed proprietary techniques for board self-testing when equipped with a SoC or FPGA, eliminating the need for costly external test benches and instruments.
