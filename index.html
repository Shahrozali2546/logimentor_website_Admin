LogiMentor

LogiMentor is a high-tech, privately held company, founded in 2014 and based in Livorno, Italy.

With over 10 years of expertise in high-performance FPGAs and their applications, LogiMentor has evolved from a small start-up to a pioneering SME, firmly established in the electronics market. It has become a leading reference point for Embedded Systems development.

LogiMentor has developed proprietary HDL Design Flow, techniques, and utilities, alongside an extensive HDL IP cores library, featuring fully verified high-performance building blocks.

What we do

A wide range of products and services

At LogiMentor, we offer a diverse array of solutions, including FPGA design services, HDL IP cores, custom designs, and IP Cores for the following industries: Radar/Sonar, Aerospace and Defense, Space, Radio Astronomy, Test and Measurement, and High-Performance Computing markets.

FPGA DESIGN

At LogiMentor, we provide top-tier FPGA Design services backed by our engineering expertise:

  • Over the past 5 years, we have successfully completed 70 diverse FPGA projects

  • Skilled in all leading vendors: 54 Xilinx projects, 11 Intel, 5 Lattice, 5 Microsemi

  • 21 projects executed on SoC platforms (Zynq and others)

We implement the following methods:

  • Proprietary VHDL coding standards

  • Automated linting and core reviews

  • Advanced verification techniques

  • Thorough and comprehensive checklists

FPGA SOLUTIONS

At LogiMentor, we develop and provide our proprietary FPGA IP CORES, independent of any vendor, optimized for speed, power, and resource efficiency, enabling seamless integration into our customers' systems. We also customize existing IP cores and design new ones to meet our customers' unique needs. We offer:

  • Over 115 IPs

  • 7 different libraries

  • 110,000 lines of code with 40,000 lines of detailed comments

Our IPs cover a wide range of functions, from simple to complex, and are ready-to-use, configurable systems. This includes everything from basic FIFO IP Cores to a fully functional ATA Over Ethernet Bridge IP.
